Our Story

Rooted in San Marzano soil, brought to life over open flame

Named for the prized tomato of Agro Sarnese-Nocerino, San Marzano is a tribute to Italy's most generous ingredient and the traditions built around it. Every pie is stretched by hand and finished in a wood-fired Neapolitan oven, in full view of the room.

From our dough — proofed for 48 hours — to the last drop of extra virgin olive oil, we hold to the old ways: honest ingredients, unhurried tables, and a warmth that feels like a nonna's kitchen.
